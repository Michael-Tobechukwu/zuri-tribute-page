Pastor E.A. Adeboye


The General Overseer, the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG), spiritual father to millions globally, inspirational leader and prolific author of over 60 books.

Famous Quote

"When in trouble, praise and thank God. To show a higher level of faith, actually, sleep off the problem. This shows you trust God to sort it out."

Timeline of his life and notable facts:

  • 1942: on March 2, he was born into a humble family in the village of Ifewara, Osun State located in the South-western part of Nigeria.
  • 1956: he was admitted into Ilesha Grammar School, Ilesha, Osun State, Western Nigeria.
  • 1967: he got married to his beloved wife, Foluke Adenike Adeboye (nee Adeyokunnu)
  • 1967: he obtained a Bachelors degree in Mathematics from the University of Ife, Nigeria.
  • 1969: he obtained a Masters degree in Hydrodynamics from the University of Lagos, Nigeria.
  • 1973: he made a personal commitment to Christ through the ministry of Reverend Josiah Olufemi Akindayomi, the founder and first general overseer of The Redeemed Christian Church of God.
  • 1975: he bagged a Doctorate Degree in Applied Mathematics also from the University of Lagos, Nigeria.
  • 1977: he was ordained a Pastor in the RCCG.
  • 1981: he became general overseer of the RCCG, aged 39.
  • 1981: Until his call into ministry, he was a mathematics professor at the University of Lagos, Nigeria.
